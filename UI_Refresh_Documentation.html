Country of residence

In order to gain an Open University qualification you will need to study a number of specified modules. If you live outside the UK and Agreed Study Areas, you may find that not all the modules required for successful completion of your chosen qualification are available. Visit the Where Can I Study pages to check whether the modules you want to study are presented where you live.

Agreed Study Areas:

Aland Islands, Andorra, Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Channel Islands, Cyprus, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Gibraltar, Greece, Hungary, Isle of Man, Italy, Latvia, Liechtenstein, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Monaco, Netherlands, Poland, Portugal, Republic of Ireland, Romania, San Marino, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland and Vatican City State
